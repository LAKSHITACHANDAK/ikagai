About This Bestselling Book:

"Ikigai" (pronounced "ee-kee-guy") is a powerful Japanese concept meaning "a reason for being." It's the intersection where your passions, talents, and what the world needs converge. This international bestseller, co-authored by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles, draws on fascinating insights from the world's longest-living people in Okinawa to bring you practical wisdom for a fulfilling life.

Through inspiring stories, actionable advice, and a deep dive into Japanese philosophies, this book guides you on a journey to uncover your own Ikigai. It's not just about finding happiness, but about discovering the underlying purpose that drives you, leading to greater well-being, contentment, and longevity.

If you've ever wondered about your place in the world, what truly makes you happy, or how to live a more meaningful existence, "Ikigai" offers a serene yet profound path forward. It's a gentle invitation to explore your inner self and align your life with your deepest values.

What You Will Learn from "Ikigai":

  • The Okinawa Blue Zone Secrets: Discover the habits and lifestyles of centenarians for health and longevity.
  • Finding Your Flow: Learn how to achieve a state of "flow" in your daily activities, leading to deeper engagement and joy.
  • The Ikigai Venn Diagram: Understand the famous framework for locating your unique reason for being.
  • Mindfulness and Resilience: Cultivate practices that enhance mental clarity and emotional strength.
  • Building Strong Communities: Explore the importance of social connections for a happy and long life.
  • Embracing Imperfection: Learn to find beauty in imperfection (Wabi-Sabi) and appreciate simple moments.
